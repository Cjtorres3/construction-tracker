Construction Cost App Requirements
Workflow: > 1. Select Project (from 'Bids' sheet).
2. Equipment Checklist (from 'Equipment' sheet).
3. Labor Entry: Checklist of names. Input Start/End times.
4. Materials/Misc Entry: Input item, quantity, and price.

Calculation Rules:

Labor Tax: Add 32% to the total daily pay.

California OT: 1.5x after 8 hours, 2x after 12 hours (No 7th-day rule).

Sick Days: Flat rate (8 hours base pay).

Budget Logic: Red (>90% bid), Yellow (50-90%), Green (<50%).

Tech Stack: Google Apps Script (HTML/CSS/JS frontend) connecting to Google Sheets API.
